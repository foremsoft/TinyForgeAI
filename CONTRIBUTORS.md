# Contributors

Thank you to all the wonderful people who have contributed to TinyForgeAI! This project exists because of your time, effort, and expertise.

## How to Get Listed

Contributors are automatically recognized when their pull requests are merged. You can also be recognized for:

- Reporting bugs and security issues
- Improving documentation
- Helping others in discussions
- Reviewing pull requests
- Creating tutorials or examples
