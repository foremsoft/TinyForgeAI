COMPANY POLICIES AND PROCEDURES
================================

1. VACATION AND TIME OFF POLICY
-------------------------------

All full-time employees are entitled to paid time off (PTO) based on their tenure:

- 0-2 years: 15 days per year
- 2-5 years: 20 days per year
- 5+ years: 25 days per year

Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.
Manager approval is required for all time off requests.

Unused PTO can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.
PTO is accrued monthly and available for use after the first 30 days of employment.

2. REMOTE WORK POLICY
---------------------

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Requirements for remote work:
- Stable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Availability during core hours (10 AM - 4 PM local time)
- Use of company-approved VPN for all work activities

Remote work arrangements must be documented in the HR system.
Fully remote positions require VP-level approval.

3. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
------------------------

Business expenses are reimbursed within 30 days of submission.

Reimbursable expenses include:
- Travel costs (flights, hotels, ground transportation)
- Meals during business travel (up to $75/day)
- Conference and training registration fees
- Work-from-home equipment (up to $500 annually)

All expenses require receipts and must be submitted within 60 days.
Expenses over $500 require pre-approval from your manager.

4. CODE OF CONDUCT
------------------

All employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Report any conflicts of interest
- Follow all applicable laws and regulations

Violations of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action.

5. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
----------------------

Performance reviews are conducted twice yearly:
- Mid-year review: June
- Annual review: December

Reviews assess performance against goals, competencies, and company values.
Self-assessments are required prior to each review meeting.

6. TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
---------------------------

The company supports continuous learning through:
- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Internal training programs and workshops
- Tuition reimbursement for approved courses (up to $5,000/year)
- Access to online learning platforms (LinkedIn Learning, Coursera)

Training requests should be discussed with your manager during 1:1 meetings.
