PRODUCT USER GUIDE
==================

Welcome to our Enterprise Data Platform! This guide covers the essential features
and how to get the most out of our product.

GETTING STARTED
---------------

1. Account Setup
   - Navigate to https://app.example.com/signup
   - Enter your work email address
   - Choose a strong password (minimum 12 characters)
   - Complete the email verification process
   - Set up two-factor authentication (required)

2. Workspace Creation
   - Click "Create Workspace" from the dashboard
   - Name your workspace (e.g., "Marketing Analytics")
   - Invite team members via email
   - Configure workspace permissions

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------

Common issues and solutions:

Q: My data connection keeps failing
A: Check that your database allows connections from our IP ranges.
   Ensure your credentials are correct and not expired.
   Verify network/firewall settings allow outbound connections on port 443.

Q: Dashboard is loading slowly
A: Consider adding filters to reduce data volume.
   Use aggregations at the database level when possible.
   Enable caching for frequently-accessed dashboards.

Q: Query results are different from expected
A: Check your date/time zone settings in workspace preferences.
   Verify join conditions and filter clauses.
   Use the query explain feature to understand execution plan.
